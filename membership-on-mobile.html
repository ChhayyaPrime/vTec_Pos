Membership on Mobile (IOS & Android)
  • Login and Registration & Support for showing Member Card
  • Supports Multi Member Card.
  • Display of reward points for each Member Card.
  • Displaying Member Card Information in 3 views: QR Code, Bar Code and Card Detail.
  • Supports to use of points at the store.
  • Supports real-time redemption
  • Display E-Coupon /E-voucher from every store & using E-Coupon /E-voucher spot at store
  • Real time process and Supports sending history of sales, earning points and redeeming points from storefront.
  • Display E-coupon / E-voucher detail in 2 views: Bar code and QR code.
  • Supports the latest 10 transactions history data display
  • Displaying news & information updates.
  • Displaying Location by connecting with Google map.
  • Supports satisfaction surveys.
